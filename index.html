About the Apartment

Located on the first floor, this 46m² apartment is ideal for a couple or a family of four. It features a spacious living room with a fully equipped kitchen, a cozy bedroom with a double bed, a modern bathroom, and a hallway. The living room sofa converts into a bed for two additional guests.

Enjoy a large **21m² terrace** overlooking a private community pool, accessible only to residents. The apartment is directly across from **De Las Naciones Park**, offering a large playground for children.

Conveniently located, it's just a **15-minute walk to Playa de Acequion Beach** and within close proximity to local shops and restaurants.

Why Choose Torrevieja?

Torrevieja is known for its stunning weather all year round, making it an excellent holiday destination. It is just **a short drive from Alicante Airport**, making travel easy and convenient.

The city boasts beautiful sandy beaches, vibrant nightlife, and plenty of activities such as shopping, golf, and nature parks.
